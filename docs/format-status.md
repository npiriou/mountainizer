It also verifies complete Type-3 fixed-header and DMA/VIF-extension framing, prop classification, model-instance resolution, linked texture previews, Type-4/5/6/7/12/13/14/15/16/18/20/21/22 structural invariants, and zero generic fallback resources in the audited assemblies.
